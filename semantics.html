about Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (born 1985) is a legendary Portuguese footballer, widely considered one of the greatest ever, known for his record-breaking goalscoring, athleticism, and major wins with clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and his current Saudi club, Al-Nassr, also captaining Portugal, with numerous Ballon d'Ors and Champions League titles, defining an era of football alongside a Brazilian Ronaldo (born 1976) who also won World Cups and FIFA awards.

Cristiano Ronaldo's achievements include 5 Ballon d'Ors, 5 Champions League titles, winning the UEFA European Championship (Euro 2016) and Nations League (2019) with Portugal, and becoming the all-time leading scorer in the Champions League and men's international football, with numerous top scorer awards and league titles in England, Spain, and Italy, solidifying his status as a legendary, record-breaking forward.
